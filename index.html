About me

Hi, I am a final-year undergraduate student in School of Information and Communication Technology, Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST). I have been working closely with Prof. Huynh Quyet Thang and Dr. Bach Le. As an undergraduate, I was selected to Talented Engineering Program (25 out of 1000 student in our cohort) at HUST

My research interests cover a broad range of topics, including but not limited to automated program repair (APR), prgram analysis, evolutionary optimization, and machine learning (ML). At present, I am also working remotely with Dr. Bach Le on applying machine learning techniques to automated program repair.
